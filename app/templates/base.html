Maintenant qui sommes-nous ?

Laissez-vous entraîner et rejoignez l’incroyable ambiance de la Gaeliste.

Welcome to our website ! You will find here a presentation of our liste and all its members. But most importantly (and it is surely what led you to click on this link), you will find out about our allôs ! You just have to select the category of allôs you’re interested in, then choose the one you desire, fill in the required information, and there you go ! your order is in preparation. Whether you live in la Meuh or not, there’s nothing simpler so don’t hesitate to ask us anything, the whole Gaeliste will be there for you.

Now, who are we ?
After a long journey across the greatest Gaelic plains and forests, the most faithful Celtic representatives are disembarking at la Meuh to initiate you all to their amazing traditions. Throughout their allôs, their afterwork, and their activities, you will discover 28 Emayans ready to warmly welcome you during the coming week in their best kilt, beret, and white shirt. With their bagpipes and their wonderful celtic songs, they will make all the Meuh dance and vibrate.
Let yourself be carried away and join the incredible atmosphere of the Gaeliste.
Take your kilt, it will be cult!
🍀The Gaeliste🍀
